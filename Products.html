Centella Light Cleansing Oil

WHAT IT IS: A lightweight, oil-based cleanser that gently melts away makeup, dirt, oils and sunscreen without stinging the eye area. KEY INGREDIENTS: Centella Asiatica Extract: Soothes and replenishes skin hydration level. HOW TO USE: Apply a few pumps of cleansing oil onto dry face with dry hands. Massage gently in circular motions. Emulsify with water to create a milky emotion before rinsing off completely with lukewarm water.
